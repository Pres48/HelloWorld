How to Play

Each turn you have limited time to pick a tile. Tiles give points, multipliers, and combos. As your level increases, time shrinks and new tile types appear.

  • Blue: Number tiles (base points)
  • Yellow: Bonus tiles (boost multiplier)
  • Green: Chain tiles (more value in sequence)
  • Red: Risk tiles (high reward, possible penalty)
